Straight Leg Extension

Position

Stand next to a table (or something else that can support when necessary).

Indication

Tilt the leg backwards (extention) as far as possible. Make sure to keep the pelvis and upper body straight. Don't make any rotation in the hip and upper body.

  • When the person stands straight, this must be indicated as 0º and the degrees when the leg stretches backwards must be calculated.
  • This exercise improves mobility of the hip.
