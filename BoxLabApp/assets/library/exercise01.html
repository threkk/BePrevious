Leg Extension

Position

Sit on a chair with both feet flat against the ground.

Indication

Stretch one knee (try to fully stretch this leg) until the shimmer above the knee and the shimmer on the ankle are on the same level. Then slowly bend the knee again to return to the first position. Next do the same with this time the other leg. There must not occur any rotation in the leg. The patient must sit upright during this exercise.

  • When the knee stretches, this must be indicated as 0º (picture B). When both feet are on the ground, this must be indicated as 90º (picture A).
  • This exercise improves the strength of the upper leg (quadriceps).
